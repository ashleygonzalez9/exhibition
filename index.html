Welcome to my website! Here is where you will be able to see my work from both projects in my World History and my English 10 class.

Coding the Past

Our first project was called coding the past.For this project we had to do many different parts for each class for example we had to interview a person and from there we created a map of the important places they talked about in the interview. We also did an essay about a topic from the interview so for me I did the essay about The Rodney King Riots. We also did a subject self portrait using photoshop that represents the person that we interviewed.
